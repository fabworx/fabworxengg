Industrial Services

Onsite Services

End-to-end field solutions: equipment erection, PEB & structure raising, piping routing & installation, plant modification, rectification and comprehensive maintenance services — executed safely, on schedule, on budget.

Equipment & Machineries Erection and Installation

FabWorx provides comprehensive plant modification and rectification services designed to enhance performance, safety, and overall operational efficiency. We evaluate existing systems with a practical engineering approach, identifying improvements that streamline processes and reduce downtime. Our modifications focus on seamless integration with ongoing operations, ensuring minimal disruption and maximum long-term benefit.

  • Heavy-equipment lifting & placement
  • Precision alignment & levelling
  • Anchor bolt grouting & base-plate fixing
  • Coupling & drive assembly

PEB and Plant Structure Erection

FabWorx designs, fabricates and erects pre-engineered buildings and equipment support structures that provide strength, stability and fast project delivery. Our solutions are built to support warehouses, sheds, conveyor gantries and pipe-racks with ease and reliability. Every structure is engineered with precision for safe, efficient and seamless integration into your facility.

  • Portal frame & rafter assembly
  • Purlin & sheeting installation
  • Equipment support towers & pipe-racks
  • High-strength bolted connections
  • Alignment survey & plumb check

Piping Routing, Erection and Installation

FabWorx delivers high-quality piping solutions that ensure smooth, safe and uninterrupted flow across industrial operations. Our services include fabrication, installation, modification and alignment of process pipelines to meet the complex needs of chemical, pharmaceutical and manufacturing plants. We focus on accuracy, durability and seamless system integration, ensuring each pipeline functions reliably under varied conditions.

  • Spool fabrication & fit-up
  • Pipe supports & hanger installation
  • Hydro-testing & flushing
  • Insulation & cladding

Plant Modification & Rectifications
