Our Story

About FabWorx Engineering

Leading manufacturer of ASME-standard process equipment with a commitment to quality, safety, and innovation. Serving pharmaceutical, chemical, and fertilizer industries across India.

Who We Are

Your Precision Engineering Excellence

FabWorx Engineering LLP was founded with a vision to provide world-class engineering solutions to India's process industries. Based in Shirwal, Satara, Maharashtra, we specialize in the design, fabrication, and installation of pressure vessels, reactors, heat exchangers, and industrial structures.

With a team of experienced engineers, certified welders, and skilled technicians, we deliver customized solutions that meet the highest international standards. Our ASME-compliant equipments and commitment to quality have made us a trusted partner for leading pharmaceutical, chemical, and fertilizer companies. With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we deliver customized, cost-effective solutions that meet the highest industry standards, delivering excellence in every project.
